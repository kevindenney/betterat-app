The model: tap = edit, long-press = reorder, swipe = delete/more, + between = insert. All four are native iOS card idioms, so the carded redesign earns them for free — no custom chrome on the card itself. Reorder & Select stay as discoverable buttons in THE WORK eyebrow for anyone who doesn't try the gesture. Matches the canonical model: reorder / insert / delete are the primary verbs, and they should feel cheap.
