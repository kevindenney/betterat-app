What changed

  • One nav bar, not two. The interest pill and the “‹ Back” row collapse into a single 46pt bar — back-affordance on the left, interest context on the right. Reclaims a whole row of vertical space.
  • Back reads as a destination. “‹ Fleets” (where you came from) instead of a bare “‹ Back”.
  • Fleet name appears once. The big hero replaces the duplicated section-label + title.
  • Stats become scannable tiles. Members / Invited / Resources as three tiles instead of a run-on “2 members · 3 invited · 0 resources · public” line; visibility moves to its own badge.
  • Owner role surfaced as a chip on the hero.
  • CTAs stay visible (Join / New fleet) directly under the actions.
