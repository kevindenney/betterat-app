Studio, minimized

Same minimalism as the workspace switcher: strip anything that repeats, label only the spine, let the list be the page. Three cuts — no Home tab, one create action, one inbox on the avatar (workspace-aware).

Cut 1 — Home

Home re-rendered the blueprint list, the threads preview, and a KPI card-grid — all of which are their own tabs. Land on Blueprints. Stats become one muted line under the title.

Cut 2 — double create

"New blueprint" (header) + "Create a new blueprint" (footer) were the same action twice. One create, sitting at the foot of the list it adds to.

Cut 3 — where "needs you" lives

No separate Studio/Admin inboxes. One inbox on the avatar, workspace-aware: Studio threads, Personal captures, Admin flags in one place. Threads stays a tab — it's where you reply.
