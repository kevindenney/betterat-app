Decisions this mock encodes

1. One control answers identity + position + jump; relation is a token inside it, not separate chrome.
2. The jump menu absorbs the minimap and gains the NOW divider + add-row — one grammar from L1 to ARC.
3. The floating pill is deleted on all platforms (NowFloat component retires; native occlusion fixed).
4. Phone drops the ‹ › arrows (swipe owns next/prev) and the word “Step” (ordinal + total suffice).
5. The stepper is level-aware: step-subject at STEP, arc-subject at ARC — no stale chips across levels.
