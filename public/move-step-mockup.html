How it behaves. "Move step…" is added to the L1 ⋯ sheet (it's the only zoom level with no drag handle). It opens a list of the current arc's steps in order, with the moving step highlighted (MOVING). Tapping any row inserts the step immediately after it; the blue line + Move here chip previews the landing slot. A leading "move to top" slot sits above row 1. On confirm it calls the same onReorder(stepId, fromIndex, toIndex) path the L3 drag list already uses, so persistence + the sort_order spine are unchanged.

Open questions for you: (a) is "after the tapped row" the right mental model, or do you prefer explicit "before / after" affordances? (b) should it scope to the whole arc, or only the undated steps between race anchors? (c) label — "Move step…" vs "Reorder…" vs "Change position".
