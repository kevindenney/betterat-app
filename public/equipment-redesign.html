Gear, not boats

Boat selection today is a sailing-only leftover (sailor_boats, boat_equipment) reachable from Settings. This redesign generalizes it to a per-interest gear primitive: boats for sailing, the bag for golf, the kit for nursing, tools & machines for a home entrepreneur — owned by the interest surface, picked contextually on steps.

📍
Where it lives: gear moves out of Settings. It's managed on the interest card in /library?zone=interests (Settings keeps account-level things only: password, notifications, units). A one-release Settings row deep-links to the new home for people who learned "My Boats" lives in Settings.

One primitive, four vocabularies

Each interest card grows a gear rail. The label, item kinds, and spec fields come from the interest's vocab — same table underneath. Primary item gets the ring + star (it's the default pick on steps).

Why not Settings?

The test: does it change per interest?
🔒Password, units, notificationsaccount-level → Settings
⛵Boats, bag, kit, toolsinterest-level → interest card
🏁Which boat for Sunday's racestep-level → picker on step
Transition affordance: Settings keeps one row — “Boats & equipment → moved to your interests” — deep-linking to /library?zone=interests, removable after a release.

Picked at point of use

Steps that involve gear get a one-tap selector pre-filled with the primary item. The selection writes a step_gear row — later, results and evidence know exactly what you used. A race step asks “which boat”; a golf practice step asks “which bag/club”.

The model underneath

Two tables and a vocab map. Interest-specific structure lives in spec jsonb, so a new vertical is a vocab entry — not a migration.

gear_items one row per thing you own

iduuidpk
user_iduuid→ users · RLS owner
interest_iduuid→ interests · gear is per-interest
kindtext'boat' 'sail' 'driver' 'stethoscope' 'machine'…
nametext"Kraken", "Stealth 2 Driver"
specjsonb{class_id, sail_number} | {loft, shaft} | {edition}
parent_iduuidnullable → gear_items · sails on boat, clubs in bag
is_primarybooldefault pick on steps · one per (user, interest, kind)
statustextactive | retired | loaned
photo_urltextnullable
acquired_ondatenullable · retired_on too
notestextfree text

step_gear what a step plans / used

step_iduuid→ steps
gear_item_iduuid→ gear_items
roletext'vessel' | 'club' | 'kit' — render hint
