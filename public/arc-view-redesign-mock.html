Arc / Season view — declutter

Today the arc view stacks 7 undifferentiated sections (header · vision · capability map · capability chips · reflections · fleet/people · librarian · THE WORK) in one flat scroll — it reads busy and every section has the same visual weight. All three options keep the same content + steps-as-cards, but differ in how they separate the four jobs: reflect on capabilities · reflect on your reflections + who helped · read & reorder the work. Vocab is interest-driven (season / arc / rotation / quarter).

A · Carded modules

Every job becomes a distinct, tinted card with its own icon + header — so the eye can tell capabilities from people from the work at a glance. Same content, just contained & separated.
