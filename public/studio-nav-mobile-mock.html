The mobile recommendation in one breath

  • 1
    Frozen header, contextual tabs. Workspace chip · Inbox bell · avatar never move. The Do·Create segment swaps the four bottom tabs and the accent color — that shared frame is what kills the jarring jump.
  • 2
    Studio owns create + mentor. Subscribers & Threads are tabs in Create mode, not menu rows. "Cohorts I mentor" / "Subscriber threads" deleted from the avatar sheet.
  • 3
    Inbox bell in the header, always. Out of the menu, one tap from any screen, badge visible everywhere.
  • 4
    "My Practice" gone from the sheet — it's the Do tab now. Keep /practice as a deep-link for marketing CTAs.
  • 5
    One Profile, Preview ⇄ Edit. Preview is the stranger's render; Edit makes the same sections live. Collapses the view-page / edit-page split.
  • 6
    Real workspace switch. Personal ⇄ JHU reloads the frame with the right data, permissions, and accent — the only switcher worth prominence.
