What changed from v1

  • 1
    "Create" → "Studio". Reuses your existing brand, no clash with the AI Coach, and holds the whole outward world (blueprints + subscribers + threads + payouts), not just "creating".
  • 2
    The Do·Create toggle is gone. The top-left context chip carries mode — Practice shows the interest, Studio reads "Studio". A whole row of vertical space comes back.
  • 3
    The chip absorbs the interest switcher. Interests nest under Practice in the one context sheet, since "which interest" only matters when you're practicing.
  • 4
    Workspace moves out of the profile menu into that same chip — so the avatar sheet shrinks to identity · profile · account.
  • 5
    Progressive disclosure: Studio only appears in the sheet once you've created or been granted a blueprint — practitioners who never author never see it.
