What I take from yesterday

Race 1 we went right because the breeze was further south than forecast and the right had pressure. We were sixth around the windward mark, third by the leeward gate, then I sailed straight into a hole on the second beat and Lemarc rolled us. Seventh.

Race 2 Rita called the left after the shift. I trusted it. Fourth across the line and we held on. The race I'm proud of is race 2.

The thing I keep returning to: I read the first shift but I didn't read the second. Bram and Mihkel both bailed left on race 1's second beat. They saw it. I didn't.
