What I take from yesterday

3 replies

First race in real chop in two months. The boat felt sticky on starboard until I dropped the traveler an inch and Sarah moved Marcus forward. Then it came alive.

The mark roundings were where we lost places. Coming in hot to a windward mark in 1.5 m of square chop is different from Marblehead summer chop. The boat wants to round up — I have to commit to the bear-away earlier.

If this is what Hong Kong looks like all week, we have work to do.
