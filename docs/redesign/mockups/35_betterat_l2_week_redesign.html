L2 Week / period — redesign
The WEEK zoom keeps its horizontal step timeline split by a NOW bar — done steps to the left, planned steps to the right — but fixes what surrounded it: the floating zoom rail that overlapped cards is now docked, the season banner becomes one period header, and the "librarian noticed" + "suggestions shaping this plan" cards merge into a single adopt menu. Frames 1–4 prove it across sailing / nursing / entrepreneur / golf; frame 5 shows adoption dropped onto the track by order.
Core move The horizontal track stays — done vs planned just reads at a glance. One continuous timeline runs left→right; a NOW bar splits it. Left of NOW: done steps, muted with a filled-green node + ✓. Under NOW: the active step, accent ring + sub-step progress. Right of NOW: planned steps, hollow dashed node, "queued". Everything else is calmer scaffolding — a docked ladder (persona-native middle label), one period header voiced by the craft's logbook/preceptor/books/coach, and one adopt menu for selective, reorderable adoption (never a bulk season import).

What changed at L2 — and why

The horizontal step timeline + NOW bar stays. What surrounded it gets calmer: a docked ladder, one period header, one adopt menu — and done-vs-planned now reads at a glance.
1Horizontal timeline kept — done vs planned differentiated. One continuous track runs left→right, split by the NOW bar. Done = muted card + filled-green node + ✓. Active = accent ring under NOW + sub-step bar. Planned = hollow dashed node + "queued". No more guessing which side of NOW a card is on.
2Docked zoom ladder, not a floating rail. All / {Arc·Rotation·Season} / Week / Step is a segmented control in its own strip — it never overlaps the track again. The middle label is persona-native, so the ladder stops leaking the sailing word "arc".
3One period header replaces the season banner. Persona pill + demoted dates + a single capacity bar (done / in-play / queued). The librarian's one-line read is the header's voice — not a separate card lower down.
4Two AI surfaces merge into one adopt menu. "Librarian noticed" + "Suggestions shaping this plan" become a single Add to this {period} menu — blueprint + cross-interest rows, each with a ＋. The nudge is the menu's framing line, not a rival card.
5Adoption drops onto the track by order. ＋ lifts one step; you drop it into a slot between cards (right of NOW) by sort_order, never onto a date. Honors "plan is a menu, not a calendar" — improvement lives in the prep slotted between anchored events.
6The validator badge carries into Review. "preceptor / coach signs off" on a queued step is the same role-typed WHO from mockup #34 — L2 shows who will confirm the capability before the step even starts.
