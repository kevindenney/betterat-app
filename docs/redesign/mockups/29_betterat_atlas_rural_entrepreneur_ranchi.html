Atlas, for a home entrepreneur near Ranchi
A sailor reads a nautical chart; a nursing student reads a coverage grid of clinical sites. A rural woman running a home business in a village outside Ranchi reads neither — her "where" is a small, known circuit of livelihood places she walks or rides between: घर (her workshop), the weekly हाट (market), the supplier, the बैंक मित्र, her सखी मंडल (self-help group), the CLF office. So this Atlas keeps the "where" lens but changes the medium to a warm landmark sketch-map — icon-first, voice-first, vernacular (Hindi here, Santhali / Sadri on localization), money in ₹ — and it leads with the one event her week turns around: the haat is her "race." Above all it keeps her eye on the payoff — more money in hand: this month's income rides on the home screen, every haat shows its expected ₹ earning, and the growth view spells out the four levers she controls — more sales, better prices, lower costs, new markets. Everything still routes through her SHG / didi trust network, never anonymous distance. (Sailing & nursing frames untouched.)

How the universal "where" lens re-charts for this craft

The reframe (per craft, the medium changes)

  • SAMEAtlas is still the "where" lens and still rides on the same platform primitives — Steps, Blueprints, Competencies/Evidence, Cohorts, the four-tier social model.
  • NEWChart → landmark livelihood circuit. Geography matters (distance to the haat, the bank, the mandi), but the surface is a small set of named, repeated places, not a continuous strategic chart (sailing) or institutional rotations (nursing). The medium that fits is a warm, icon-first sketch-map.
  • NEWThe haat is the recurring event = the "race." A time-and-place-bound market day she prepares for, shows up to, and reviews — so it gets the cockpit (gauges + plan + last-result), recolored for selling.
  • PAYOFFMoney is the spine, not a footnote. The end she's chasing is income, so it's visible everywhere: month-to-date earnings on the home screen, an expected-₹ figure on every haat, a target-vs-last band in the cockpit, and a growth view built entirely around the four levers she controls — more sales, better prices (marketing/branding), lower costs, new markets. Each lever carries a concrete ₹/month impact and a where hook, and they sum to the gap that makes her लखपति.

Designed for the real user (not a translation of a US app)

  • VOICEVoice-first + audio on every card. Low/variable literacy and shared phones mean the 🎙️ बोलें mic is the primary input and 🔊 read-aloud is everywhere — typing is the fallback, not the default.
  • VERNVernacular, icon-first, big touch targets. Hindi here; Santhali (Ol Chiki) / Sadri / Mundari / Kurukh are the localization targets for the Ranchi belt. Numerals and money in ₹; UPI-native.
  • EDGEOffline-tolerant & shared-phone aware. Haat calendar, prices and the circuit cache locally and sync when there's signal; no sensitive financial detail exposed on a shared handset.
  • TRUSTSHG / didi network is the social fabric. Discovery is "your समूह," not anonymous nearby strangers — matching how JSLPS / DAY-NRLM livelihoods actually organize.

Four-tier model → this persona's vocabulary

Interest (add)घर उद्योग — running a home enterprise (achar/papad, tussar silk, lac, sal-leaf plates, mushroom, tailoring…)
Organization (join)her SHG / सखी मंडल → VO → CLF federation, under JSLPS / DAY-NRLM
Program (subscribe)लखपति दीदी pathway, JSLPS livelihood training, PMFME food-processing scheme, Mudra credit
People (follow)दीदी peers in her समूह + the CRP / Sakhi mentors (Aajeevika / Bank / Pashu / Krishi Sakhi) and Cluster Coordinator

Primitive mapping

  • Step = a unit of her enterprise — source mango, sterilize jars, make a batch, label, sell at haat, deposit savings. A Blueprint = a CLF's "achar enterprise" plan she adopts step by step (the Plan-is-a-menu pattern).
  • Competency / Evidence → livelihood capability (sourcing · making/quality · selling · money · digital), evidenced by real sales, savings entries, a repaid loan, an FSSAI number — not a checkbox. In Frame C these capabilities are surfaced as the income levers that move them: a "weak selling" capability becomes the more-sales / new-market lever with a ₹ figure attached. Means (capability) expressed as ends (money).
  • Gap routing is spatial-economic: an underserved haat where didis already go, a subsidy at the CLF — the "where" makes the next move concrete.

Build order (isolated from sailing & nursing surfaces)

  • E0 — Coherence: per-interest home geography (→ Ranchi block), vernacular + audio shell, ₹/UPI formatting, SHG-scoped Nearby. Biggest "this is built for me" win.
  • E1 — Landmark map & circuit (Frame A) · E2 — Haat cockpit + last-haat ledger (Frame B) · E3 — Business health / Lakhpati + gap routing (Frame C) · E4 — Curated JSLPS / CLF partner & scheme layer.
  • Data caveat: needs an SHG→VO→CLF org structure, a haat calendar (market day + location per village), a simple sales/savings ledger, and a scheme catalog (PMFME, Mudra, Lakhpati). Verify what can be seeded for a Jharkhand demo persona before committing — this is the rural-Indian-entrepreneur slot in the per-persona Atlas rollout.
