Watch tab — three redesign directions
Today's Watch tab has three problems: the filter pills say "Following" while the body shows cohort threads (label ≠ content), there are two separate "Following" entry points (the header count row and a pill), and the cards lead with the person, not the step — so the "adapt anything useful into your own practice" promise has no affordance where your eyes land. All three directions below make the step the headline, add a one-tap Adapt, and replace the mismatched pills with a lens segment whose label always matches the feed.
