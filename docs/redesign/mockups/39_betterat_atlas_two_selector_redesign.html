Atlas — lead with whose steps, not search
Corrected priority: search isn't the hero of Atlas. The two jobs that are: seeing the steps of people you follow & know (Crew · Fleet · Following) and mapping your own steps. So the relationship lens leads — search demotes to a small icon. We still keep the rest of the review's fixes: one step picker (the Saved ★ dropdown, no more "Pick another"), a de-crowded bar, capture on a FAB, and place-select → detail sheet.
Why not search-firstGoogle Maps leads with search because its job is finding a destination. Atlas's job is different: your steps on the map, plus the steps of people you follow & know. So the hero is the relationship strip — My steps on by default, then Crew · Fleet · Following — and search shrinks to a 🔍 icon beside Saved and your profile. We still borrow Google's scrollable chip strip, Saved ★ favorites dropdown (= the one step picker), and place-detail sheet; we just don't put a search field where the lens should be.

What changed & why

The two fixes you called out

  • FIXDuplicate step picker removed. "Pick another" is gone from the bottom card. The single picker is the Saved ★ dropdown in the top bar (Frame B) — the one you said to keep. The card's job shrinks to: show the focused step + Open step.
  • FIXTop bar de-crowded. Was: interest ▾ · org ▾ · search · layers · + · inbox · avatar (7). Now: context · 🔍 · ★ · avatar (4). Inbox badge sits on the avatar; org switching folds into the context chip; Layers moves onto the chip strip; ＋ capture becomes a FAB.
  • FIXSearch is no longer the hero. You called it: search isn't key — seeing the steps of people you follow & know, and mapping your own, is. Search drops to a 🔍 icon; the relationship strip takes the headline slot.

What we borrowed from Google Maps (minus search-first)

  • NEWRelationship strip is the hero (where Google puts its category row): My steps · Crew · Fleet · Following | Races · Layers. My steps on by default (maps your steps); add others' steps by tapping their dot-coded chip. WHOSE leads, the WHAT/Races filter follows after a hairline divider.
  • NEWFavorites dropdown → Saved & jump: the jump list is grouped by relationship (My steps · Crew · Fleet · Following · Nearby · Groups, dot-coded to the lens strip), then Your racing areas and Saved venues in the same menu. This absorbs the old standalone Nearby list — finding the steps of people you follow & know now lives in one place. Saving a place from the detail sheet drops it here.
  • NEWInbox folds onto the avatar — everywhere. The standalone bell is gone; the unread count rides as a red badge on the profile avatar, and the menu carries an Inbox row. Adopted on every profile-bearing surface (Atlas, Practice, Library, Watch), not just here — one less icon in every top bar.
  • NEWSelect a place → detail panel. Google's left sidebar becomes a mobile bottom sheet: cover photo, serif title, provenance, and an action row (Open · Fly to · Save · Share). The compact callout is the glance; drag up for the full sheet.
