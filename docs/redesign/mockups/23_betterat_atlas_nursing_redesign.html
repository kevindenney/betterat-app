Atlas, re-charted for nursing students
A sailor reads a map as a cockpit — the geography is the strategy. A nursing student's "where" isn't continuous water; it's a handful of named clinical sites, and the resolution that matters (which unit) is exactly the one HIPAA forbids putting on a map. So the nursing Atlas inverts the sailing one: it leads with a structured Sites surface — rotation journey, competency coverage by site, who from your cohort is there — and demotes the street map to a secondary toggle. Same "where" lens; the medium that fits the craft is a coverage grid, not a chart. (Sailing frames F1–F3 are untouched.)

Per-surface assessment & what changed

What's broken today (from the screenshots)

  • FIXNearby leaks sailing into the nursing frame. The sheet header reads WITHIN 25KM · HONG KONG - VICTORIA HARBOR and lists yacht clubs + demo-markus@regattaflow.app · Victoria Harbour · start line bias · fleet. Root cause: DiscoverNearbyContent anchors to the user's sailing home venue via useUserHomeVenue and isn't interest-scoped. Fix: anchor to the active frame center (Baltimore) and pass interestSlug='nursing' into useAtlasPeerSteps.
  • FIX"Log shift · Coming soon" is a stub. The core nursing loop is unbuilt (roadmap Phase A.3). Without it the coverage grid can never fill in. Ship the real site-level log-shift flow (reuse the healthcare write-lint + the already-fail-closed privacy gate).
  • FIXWalk-time annotations are sailing-think. "2 min / 3 min" connectors between JHH, Pinkard, Sibley borrow the sailor's distance/layline grammar. No nurse optimizes hospital-to-hospital walking — remove them from the nursing frame.
  • FIXNEXT pill is a hardcoded fixture. "JHH 4 South · cardiac" is static; wire it to the student's real upcoming rotation via a nursing next_event resolver.
  • FIXHome geography is global. The persona's home venue is Hong Kong (from sailing), so nursing opens on the wrong continent. Per-interest home_geography_resolver: sailing → HK, nursing → Baltimore.

The core move: chart → coverage

  • NEWSites-first home (Frame A) — structured rotation list as the default surface; map demoted to a segment toggle.
  • NEWCompetency coverage by site (Frames A/B) — every site shows what skills it's given you, colored by cluster.
  • NEWCompetency constellation + gap view (Frame C) — "where have I evidenced X / where am I short before graduation," routed to the upcoming rotation that closes the gap.
  • NEWReal Log-a-shift loop (Frame B) — site-level, no patient text; the thing that earns coverage instead of seeding it.
  • NEWCohort presence, date-based — "5 cohort-mates here this block," never real-time / "currently here."
  • NEWMap toggle as a node map, not a chart (Frame A2) — when the demoted street map is shown it draws a handful of building pins (status-colored, week-stamped), a dashed rotation-journey line in term order, and lifts the gap badge onto the rotation that closes a missing competency. Two things a list can't express — the commute geography and the journey-as-timeline — are its only job; everything else routes back to the Sites surface. Privacy floor is structural: site-level only, no unit/room, no individual live position.

What's already right (keep)

  • KEEPCohort relationship primitive + You · Cohort · Program · Faculty · Following chips. Fleet/Crew dropped for nursing.
  • KEEPSite-level privacy floor, fail-closed gate, healthcare write-lint. The structured surface makes this easier — a clinical step renders as "a step at this site," inherently site-level.
  • KEEPCohort heatmap aggregate (no individual patient sites) — reused as the in-detail "cohort this week" card.
  • KEEPAtlas as the universal "where" lens — the medium just changes per craft: a chart for sailors, a coverage grid for nurses.

Build order (isolated from sailing F1–F3)

  • N0 — Coherence fixes: interest-scope Nearby, per-interest home geography, strip walk-times, real next-rotation resolver. Low risk, biggest "this isn't sailing" win.
  • N1 — Sites-first view (Frame A) · N2 — Real Log Shift (Frame B) · N3 — Constellation + gaps (Frame C) · N4 — Curated JHU partner layer.
  • Data caveat: N1/N3 need a rotation/assignment concept + competency-by-site coverage (competency_attempts ⋈ step_location → site POI). Verify what's seeded for the demo persona before committing.
