Sail-racing step design · Step vs Race selection
A full step composer for the sailing interest. Every step shares the same skeleton — what / how / why / who / when. The first field is a Step ⟷ Race selector. Choosing Race reveals the on-water grammar (course & marks, conditions) that Atlas treats specially; a plain Step never shows it.
ModelWe don't have step types — we have steps. The single distinction that changes behavior is race vs not-race, because a race carries a venue, course geometry, marks and on-water conditions an ordinary step doesn't. One is_race flag (set here, when planning) replaces Phase M's keyword resolver. Race steps render ⛵ blue inside their race-area polygon on Atlas; every other step is a neutral status dot.
