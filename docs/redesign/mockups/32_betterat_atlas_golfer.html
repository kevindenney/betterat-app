How the universal "where" lens re-charts for golf

Your questions, answered

  • QDo courses, sims & ranges replace racing areas + the race course? Yes. Venues (home club, courses played, driving range, putting green, simulator) are the golf analog of sailing's racing areas, and the single-course hole-map is the analog of the race course — same Sites|Map shell, reskinned.
  • QWhat makes it great for golfers (vs. a copy of sailing)? Golf's "where" is discrete & repeated (a handful of named places) and hole-by-hole, so skill is spatially located. That unlocks the strokes-gained heat map per hole (Frame B) and located leaks (Frame C: "approach bleeds from 150–175y", "putting worst on fast greens") — a coach's-eye view a continuous nautical chart can't give.
  • QThe simulator gets special treatment. It's weather-independent, data-rich (Trackman / launch-monitor), and can virtually play a course you'll compete on — so it's both a practice venue (Frame A/D) and the destination the gap-router sends you to (Frame C).

The reframe (per craft, the medium changes)

  • SAMEAtlas is still the "where" lens on the same platform primitives — Steps, Blueprints, Competencies/Evidence, Cohorts, the four-tier social model — and the same Sites | Map shell from the nursing redesign.
  • NEWChart → venues + a hole-by-hole course map. A small set of named, repeated places (club, courses, range, green, sim), and — unique to golf — a course map where your scoring heat is plotted per hole.
  • NEWThe round / tournament is the "race." A time-and-place-bound event she prepares for, plays, and reviews — so it gets the cockpit (conditions + game plan + last round), in golf vocab (wind, Stimp, pins, fairways/GIR/putts).

Primitive mapping

  • Step = a round, lesson, range session, sim session, or tournament. A Blueprint = a coach's or club's training plan she adopts step by step (Plan-is-a-menu).
  • Competency / Evidence → strokes-gained categories (off-the-tee · approach · short game · putting · course management), evidenced by real scores, fairways/GIR/putts, and launch-monitor data — not checkboxes. Frame C is the strokes-gained constellation, and every category is located on the course.
  • Gap routing is spatial: a leak (putting speed, 150–175y wedges) routes to a place (sim putting lab, range station) and a person (the club pro) — the "where" makes the next practice concrete.

Build order (isolated from sailing, nursing & entrepreneur surfaces)

  • G0 — Coherence: golf venue types (course / range / sim / green) + fairway register + Sites|Map shell for the golf frame. Reuses the shared Atlas shell; golf specifics go in the frame + resolvers + vocab.
  • G1 — Venues & next-round hero (A) · G2 — Course hole-map + round cockpit (B, the keystone — the located scoring heat is the golf "wow") · G3 — Handicap + strokes-gained located + gap routing (C) · G4 — Nearby tee-times / group / coach + lesson & sim booking (D).
