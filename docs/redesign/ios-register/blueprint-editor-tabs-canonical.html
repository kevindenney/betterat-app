Frame 5 of the prior pass shipped the blueprint editor’s cover. These six frames finish the editor — the left sidebar shifts into Editing mode (the org-switcher tucks under a navy back-banner; the section nav becomes a per-blueprint outline) and the body switches per sub-tab. The tenant on screen is Johns Hopkins School of Nursing; the blueprint is Sepsis bundle recognition, authored by Dr. R. Murphy, assigned to BSN Class of 2027 — Cohort A.

Navy #28406B is the only product-chrome accent on this surface — primary buttons, eyebrows, active-tab underline, coverage-strength fills. Status pills are tone-tinted (draft warn, published green). All counts tabular. Cards group at 22px.
