Capability evidence as endorsements, not XP

The five-dot indicators (emerging → developing → competent → fluent → expert) are deliberately restrained. No bars, no levels-up animation, no streak counters. Evidence is the user's own quoted words plus a trail across Plan, Do, Reflect. A flagged gap notes that it informs the next Plan — but doesn't count toward a level. The model is LinkedIn endorsements through deliberate practice, not gamified progression.

Carry forward closes the loop

The final card is the only place the surface gets visual weight. A subtle blue indicator at its top edge, a thin blue line under the title, a primary Plan next step → CTA — this is the bridge from one cycle to the next. Two bullets only: one watch-for (carrying a flag), one build-on (carrying capability momentum). This is the surface that makes the practice loop feel like a loop, not isolated steps.
