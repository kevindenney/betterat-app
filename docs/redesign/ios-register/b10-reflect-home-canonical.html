Anatomy · 393 × 852 · callouts A–G

Same letter convention as B.7: A is the state pill, F/G are the primary CTA and its hint. Purple is Reflect's exclusive accent — never coral, never green.

1 · The contemplative voice lives in the type, not the page.

Legacy Reflect leaned on a warm stage-color background and a borderless wash to feel quiet. The re-skin moves to white-card-on-gray iOS chrome — the same chrome the rest of the product uses — and trusts the serif body, italic vision excerpt, and italic-with-provenance moments list to do the contemplative work. The test holds: read the surface and ask whether it feels calm. If yes, the chrome shift cost nothing.

serif body · italic vision · italic moments

2 · Purple is Reflect's ring-fenced accent.

Do has coral. Plan has blue. Trophy has green. Reflect gets purple — the long-arc, AI-handoff accent. It appears on the state pill, the Vision-evolution card's wash, and the synthesis eyebrow. Nowhere else on the screen. If anything new tries to claim purple — feature highlight, badge, AI hint — it goes through review the same way coral does on Do.

state pill · vision card · synthesis eyebrow

3 · Capture a reflection is one tap away, always.

Reflect is a reading surface, but reading frequently prompts writing. The single iOS-blue mic-led CTA at the foot of the card makes that latency near-zero, without claiming visual real estate inside the contemplative read. The text-only re-synthesize the year link sits underneath as the work-mode escape hatch — present, but quiet.

primary CTA · ti-microphone · ios-blue

4 · Sketchbook arcs survive the re-skin.

The line-drawing capability arcs are load-bearing for the surface's character — if they read as sparkline tiles, the chrome has won. The re-skin keeps them as hand-drawn 1.1-px Béziers with three dashed state guides and two endpoint dots. They're now housed inside small white tiles (radius 12, 0.5 px gray-5 border) so they sit comfortably on the card; the drawing itself is unchanged.

1.1 px arc · 0.6 px dashed guides · 2 endpoint dots

5 · What survived from legacy.

Year-so-far paragraph (serif body, sentence-case eyebrow, sans provenance). Vision evolution with quiet enter affordance. Where-your-thinking-has-shifted excerpt. Capability arcs strip. Moments-returned-to as italic-serif-with-provenance. Coming-up footer as sans prose. All of it — structure, copy, hierarchy — preserved. The only content change is voice on the new chrome elements (state pill, step strip, CTA copy).

structure · copy · hierarchy — preserved

6 · What's new from the B.7 register.

White card with iOS-native shadow on a light-gray ground. State pill in the card header pairing a glyph + caps eyebrow. Two-stat block (Wks to Worlds, Races in) on the right of the header — the same 17/600 tnum pattern used for Captures / Elapsed on Do. Step strip naming Spring Series. Footer CTA strip pinned at card bottom with primary + text hint. Same letter convention (A–G) on the anatomy frame.

state pill · stats · step strip · footer CTA
