The Plan tab across empty, partial, and filled — AI Coach starts prominent, recedes as the user takes over

One step (Light-air starts in shifty breeze), one tab (Plan), three completion states. The progression isolates a single design question: how does the AI Coach affordance behave as the plan fills in? Answer — full card at the top while the body is empty, a small persistent link once the user has begun, never gone entirely. The phase tab itself earns a green check only when every required field is populated.

AI Coach recedes, never leaves

The AI Coach card carries maximum visual weight when the body is empty — it is the recommended first move. Once any field is non-empty, the card collapses to a "Continue with AI Coach →" link pinned at the top of the body. The user can always re-engage, but the affordance is no longer competing with their own writing.

iOS blue · linear-gradient tinted card → small link · same icon throughout

Plan ready — earned, not declared

The phase tab earns a small green check only when What, all sub-steps, Why, and at least one capability are present. No "Submit" button; no progress bar. The check is the smallest possible cue that the loop can advance into Do. Saving is continuous and ambient — a quiet "Saved" next to the AI Coach link.

system green · 14×14 · circular check · phase-tabs gutter only

Capabilities auto-tag from the plan

Capability chips populate from the sub-step tags as the user writes — Starts and Weather reading filled (high confidence), Boat tune outlined (inferred but lower confidence, awaiting confirmation). Cross-interest relevance (Nursing → "Communication under pressure") surfaces in More Options, never blocking the primary flow.
