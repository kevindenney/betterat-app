The peek is a silhouette

Adjacent step cards bleed off both edges of the timeline area. What's visible is edge + corner + shadow only — no title, no content, no metadata. The peek implies "more here" without trying to read.

Why so empty: the peek does spatial work, not informational work. If a user wants to know what step 5 was, they swipe or tap; they don't squint at a sliver.

Two gestures, two scopes. Horizontal swipe pages between steps. Tap inside the card switches phase. No conflict.

Phase tabs are always all three

No gating, no progressive disclosure. Plan, Do, and Reflect are always tappable, even when empty. A user can land on Reflect to read the prompts before they've done any of the Do work; that's a valid use.

The green dot on a phase tab marks "this phase has content the user produced." It is ambient context, not a gate.

  • ActiveiOS blue label, blue underline, 0.5px hairline above tab strip.
  • Donelabel in standard label color, 6px green dot prefix.
  • Emptylabel in tertiary gray, no decoration.

State in the head, not the chrome

The card's step-label row above the title carries the temporal state of this practice cycle. Frame 1 reads Current step in iOS blue. Frame 2 reads Live · in progress with the coral live dot from the On-the-Water register. Frame 3 reads Race complete · debrief open in tertiary gray.

Same grammar, three weights. The header strip and the tab bar don't have to know; they just render. Surface decides weight; chrome stays still.

Profile is the credential the loop produces

Every Reflect-phase capability tag (Frame 3, coral evidence row) accumulates against the user's Profile. A capability shows progress dots and an evidence count linking back to the source steps.

Some capabilities are progress-shaped — five dots filling over time. Some are credential-shaped — a certification rosette next to references. Rules knowledge in Frame 4 is the credential case.

The point of Practice is Profile. The loop on the left exists to feed the artifact on the right.

Timeline geometry

  • Center card318pt wide on a 393pt iPhone — 81%. Min height 612pt to feel "page-sized."
  • Peek cards268pt wide, 14pt gap from center. ~28pt of each peek visible after centering and clipping.
  • Card radius22pt — between the 14pt iOS Notes card radius and the 28pt Wallet pass radius. Reads as "a thing," not "a row."
  • Shadowtwo-layer: 1pt hairline directly below + 18pt 38pt blur for depth. Peeks get a quieter version.

Phase content density

  • Planchecklist with capability tags. Items can be checked off; tags drive the Profile aggregation. The + Add to plan row is the only composer affordance.
  • Doreverse-chronological log. Voice (italic), typed (regular), photo (thumbnail), silent-flag (coral pill) — same vocabulary as the editorial On-the-Water register.
  • Reflectthree prompt blocks (What worked / What didn't / What would you do differently) plus the coral evidence-tag affordance and a soft auto-summary route.

  • Practice-engine identityflag-3 icon (filled when active); visible label is interest vocabulary (`Race` in these Sail Racing frames).
  • Playbookbook-2 icon. The library.

  • Tab bar surfaceiOS standard light material — 82% white over the gray-6 ground, 28pt blur, 0.5pt top hairline. 84pt tall including the safe-area inset.
