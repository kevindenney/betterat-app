The AI Coach conversation inside the Plan tab — empty plan, takeover, draft inline, plan filled

The conversational surface that lets the user describe a goal in natural language and watch the Plan fields (What · How · Why · Capabilities) drafted in front of them. This is the in-step refinement path, distinct from the step-creation path in Add Step Flow · Canonical — the user has already created the step; now they are filling out, refining, or rewriting its Plan tab over time. The takeover presents over the Plan tab, captures four turns, emits an inline draft card, and dismisses back to a populated Plan with AI Coach receded to a small link.

One step, one card, four moments

The four frames document a single continuous loop on the same step. The Plan tab never becomes a form to fill; it becomes a conversation to have. The takeover is a presented-modally surface — slides up over the Plan, dismissed by Cancel or by accepting the draft. The user can always re-enter via the persistent "Continue with AI Coach" link in the filled state.

The draft is a Plan preview, not a form

The inline draft card in Frame 3 mirrors the Plan tab's field structure exactly — What · How · Why · Capabilities — at ~80% scale, with a small DRAFT chip tagged with the sparkle glyph. Capability chips obey the same filled / outlined / dashed-empty vocabulary as the canonical. Two iOS buttons: Accept & add to plan (blue primary) or Keep refining (text). The conversation continues below — accept is committal, not terminal.

Conversation collapses, plan persists

On accept, the takeover dismisses and AI Coach recedes to a single line at the top of the body — same glyph, same word, smaller weight. A short toast confirms "Plan drafted with AI Coach · refine anytime". The phase tab earns a green check, capabilities auto-tag from the sub-steps (Starts · Weather reading · Boat tune), and the cross-interest suggestion (Nursing → "Communication under pressure") appears in More Options. No "Submit"; the plan is finished when it looks finished.
